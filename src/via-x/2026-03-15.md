### [@mstockton](https://x.com/mstockton/status/2033194970329325814)

Matt Stockton on bootstrapping AI skills: "Getting a model injected with the right 'correct context' at the beginning of its context window matters a lot… use the models to help you bootstrap your skills for them. It's also important to really reflect on what you want the skills to do, and write that down first — before you engage the models, because once you engage the models, there is this temptation to let it do all of your thinking for you."

> The warning about letting models do your thinking is the part worth sitting with. The trap is real — it's easy to end up with competent-sounding output that doesn't actually reflect what you wanted. Writing down your intent first, before the model touches it, keeps the design yours.
