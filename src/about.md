# About

I'm Vin. I build things with software and think about how AI changes everything.

By day I work in enterprise tech. By night I build — tools that solve real problems for real people. I believe the best software comes from scratching your own itch, and that AI is the most exciting thing to happen to builders since the internet.

Interests: AI, software architecture, anthropology, history, paleontology, astronomy, art.

This site is my notebook — short takes on things I find interesting, longer essays when something deserves it. Think of it as a conversation I'm having with the internet.
